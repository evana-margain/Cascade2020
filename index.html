About Us
We are a calm company

At Cascade, we establish healthy professional environments that not only allow, but encourage us to do conscious, focused and energetic work. By truly caring and protecting our team, we bring a sense of belonging that most companies can only dream of. This turns into happy, involved professionals ready to do the work of their lives.

Our Mission

To create competitive, valuable technical services in Staff Augmentation | Mobile Development | Website Development | Backend Services Development | Application Maintenance | E-Commerce | UI Design | UX Design | Project & Delivery Management | Product Discovery, authentic to our clients’ own vision and values.

Our Vision

To guide with a clear sense of identity and purpose, inspiring both clients and the industry around us to achieve effective, calmer practices. Through experience, hard work, and passion, we strive to innovate in the way this industry functions and create better experiences for our customers, users, and stakeholders, adding value to their business models.

Our Values

01

Focus On Strengths

We help our customers develop ambitious projects with founder-market-product-fit. To lead by example, we focus on our specific knowledge –those skills we’re naturals at since childhood, such as coding and designing.

02

Closeness To Nature

When the job is done, we leave our devices and work at home, to focus on the other side of the human experience – that of sensing and understanding nature. It's how we rest our minds, recover peace and clarity, so we can come back to the office in sharp form.

03

Love For Reading

Books are the best technology, since they let us enter some of the brightest minds. Reading is how we overcome dogma, how we stop being blind followers and become critical students; gaining new ideas, skills and perspectives that make of our products something great.

04

Healthy Work Environment

We do our best work and influence others by building a profitable, successful company based on calm professional practices. We do this by choosing fair compensations, less interdependencies, stability, and carefully crafted perks so our teams can put their health, family and personal time first.
